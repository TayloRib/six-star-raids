Notes on the Raid Typing Chart:

Just because a Pokémon seems to be a good match-up, Raid Pokémon can have movesets with typing outside of their main type that can be super effective against something that seems like a safe bet.

Also, this is purely from a defensive standpoint. A good match up will keep your raid Pokémon alive, but in order to defeat it, you'll have to match your moveset type up with that Pokémons tera typing.

This is a general guide to give you a good starting point. Feel free to adjust your strategies after getting more experience with each raid Pokémon.
